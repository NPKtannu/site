    Our Story

    NPK Agric is the one stop solution for commmercial farming in Ranchi(Jharkhand).

    In 2007, we started the agriculture firm named as, NPK Agric with Netafim, we are selling seeds, fertilizers all types of agriculture products. When we started this firm we understood the needs of the farmer, value of a farm for a farmer and thus we provide them with best we can because we just grow their business but they grow our needs.
    Our objective and planning are always focused on our customer needs & benefits, no matter where we are but our presence is in each drop of water and in every growing crop.
    Today we are not only providing our products and services but we are also providing the farmers with the knowledge and benfits of our products and are providing training to them for better future.

    Our Mission

    In 2009, we proudly introduce our self as the only company to have successfully supplied and installed more than 300 FDS (A Gravity based 1000sq mtr. irrigation system with 12mm pressure compensated drip line) in the state of Jharkhand, changing the life of our state's poorest farmers with dedication, technology and most importantly honesty.
